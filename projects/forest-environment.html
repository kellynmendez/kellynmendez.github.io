Stylized Forest Virtual Environment

I made this environment over the course of seven weeks this past summer as my first project in an introductory virtual environments class. All of the assets were rendered in Maya, textured in Substance Painter, Photoshop, and Procreate, and rendered in Unreal 5.

For this project, I made a few assets every week and used the last week or so for scene composition, lighting, and post-processing. Because this was a school project, most of the assets I made were assigned to me every week (barrel, fence, lampost, rocks, and all foliage), and at the end we got to choose what environment we wanted to make with those assets. I ended up making a homey forest path, and in the final couple of weeks I added the book and tree stump as my key asset to create that cozy feeling.

This project was a very fun introduction to environment art, and the thing I enjoyed the most was learning how to hand paint textures in both Photoshop and Procreate to make my assets stylized. The most rewarding part of this project for me was seeing how the assets came together to make a full scene at the end of the seven weeks.
