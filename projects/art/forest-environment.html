Homey Forest Path

I made this environment over the course of seven weeks in the summer of 2022. All of the assets were rendered in Maya, textured in Substance Painter, Photoshop, and Procreate, and rendered in Unreal 5.

For this project, I made a few assets every week and used the last week or so for scene composition, lighting, and post-processing. This project was a very fun introduction to environment art, and the thing I enjoyed the most was learning how to hand paint textures in both Photoshop and Procreate to give my assets a more stylized look. In the future, I want to create an art bible for my projects because I didn't really set a specific style I was going for when I started this project, and I want to be able to define a style well in the future and be consistent with it.
