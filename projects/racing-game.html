Enchanted Forest Racing Game

This project was completed over the course of the Fall 2021 semester for my Animation and Game Fundamentals class. It was split into two parts, the creation of the introduction scene and the creation of the game itself.

Intro Scene

Over the course of six weeks, I went through each stage of production: modeling, surfacing, rigging, animation, and lighting. All stages were done in Maya, and surfacing texture files were created in Photoshop.

Game Demo

Over the last six weeks, I completed the full racing game. All scripts were imported, as were the trees and rocks, but everything else I did myself. I designed the level and the environment of the game, which included modeling and texturing the toadstool houses and crystals. Finally, I set up all lighting, particle systems, animations, and audio.
